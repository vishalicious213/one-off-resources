## Notes

- Think about how you would go about hiring a plumber or another service person when you're job hunting
    - Who goes to the top of your list?
        - The person with the referral from someone you know
        - The person who responded quickly, had a body of work
        - These are the people submitting proposals & getting reviews & referrals
    - People at the bottom of the list are the ones with no proof (reviews, referrals)
        - Even if they give you 10% off, if they have no proof, they don't get hired
        - Errors cost money
        - This is the person who just clicked apply

### Hitlisting

- Only 30% of jobs get posted to job boards

    - Most will be on platforms where they get innundated with spam, you can't stand out
    - You're fist-fighting for 10% of jobs in a mosh pit

    - m.Campos ‚Äî It's really more about the process to get to the 60 referrals. The number is daunting but what you do to get there is what will get you the job

    - Add to hitlist
    - Follow on Twitter
    - Find their email
    - Ask for coffee chat
    - Send a thank you

    - Be more friendly with the hiring manager & engineer

- Companies that were hiring in the past year are probably going to keep hiring
